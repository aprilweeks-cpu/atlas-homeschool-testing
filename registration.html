Before You Register

Please have the following information ready:

  • Student’s legal first and last name
  • Student’s grade level
  • Parent/guardian contact information
Important:
Registration must be completed by the deadline listed on the homepage.
Students who are not registered by the deadline will not be able to participate in ATLAS testing for this administration.

What Happens After You Register?
