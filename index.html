Welcome

This website provides everything homeschool families need to register for ATLAS testing, prepare for testing day, and access results.

Please read all information carefully before registering your student.

Important Dates

  • Registration Opens: January
  • Registration Deadline: February
  • Testing Window: March–April
  • Score Reports Available: May

Important:
Students must be registered by the deadline to participate. Late registrations cannot be accepted.

Before Testing

  • Testing device with ATLAS Secure Browser
  • Working camera and microphone
  • Quiet, distraction-free environment
  • Blank scratch paper
  • Approved calculator (if allowed)

Testing Day Expectations

  • Students must follow all Test Administrator directions
  • Camera and microphone must remain on during testing
  • No outside help may be given during testing
  • Testing sessions have a scheduled stop time

After Testing

  • Scores will be available through the ATLAS Family Portal
  • Families will receive instructions when reports are ready
  • Keep a copy of your student’s Individual Student Report (ISR)
