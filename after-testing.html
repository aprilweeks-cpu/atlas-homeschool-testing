Score Reports

ATLAS score reports (Individual Student Reports) will be available after the testing window closes.

Important:
Scores are not available immediately after testing. Families will be notified when reports are ready.

How to Access Scores

  • Scores will be available through the ATLAS Family Portal
  • Instructions for accessing reports will be provided when results are released
  • Please retain a copy of your student's score report for your records

What to Expect

  • Score reports include performance levels and scale scores
  • Results may be used to help inform instructional decisions
  • Homeschool participation does not require additional follow-up
