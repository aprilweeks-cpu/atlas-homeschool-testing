What You Need Before Testing

  • A computer or laptop capable of running the ATLAS Secure Browser
  • Reliable internet connection
  • Working camera and microphone
  • A quiet, distraction-free testing environment
  • Blank scratch paper
  • Approved calculator (if allowed for the test)

Technology Requirements

  • The ATLAS Secure Browser must be installed and working prior to testing
  • Students must be able to access camera and microphone when prompted
  • School-issued devices are recommended when available

Testing Environment

  • Students must test in a quiet, distraction-free location
  • The testing area should be free of unauthorized materials
  • No additional screens or devices may be in use during testing

Parent and Student Responsibilities

  • Parents may assist with logging in and setup only
  • Students must complete all test questions independently
  • No coaching, prompting, or assistance is allowed during testing
Important:
Providing assistance during testing may invalidate the student's assessment.

Before Test Day

  • Verify that your device meets all requirements
  • Test your camera and microphone
  • Ensure your testing space is ready
  • Review all instructions provided prior to testing

For detailed instructions about what will happen during testing, please review the Testing Day page.
