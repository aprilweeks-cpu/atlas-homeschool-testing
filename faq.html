Registration

Who can participate in ATLAS homeschool testing?

Students participating in the Education Freedom Account Program in grades 3–10 who are registered by the deadline.

What happens if I miss the registration deadline?

Students who are not registered by the deadline cannot be added to the testing system for the current administration. Your student can take another approved norm referenced test to fulfill their EFA testing requirements.

Testing

What devices are approved for testing?

See this resource for approved devices and operating systems.

What do students need on testing day?

Students need a testing device, internet access, camera, and any allowed materials (scratch paper, calculator).

After Testing

When will scores be available?

For home school students, scores are typically available in the ATLAS Family Portal after the testing window has closed. Instructions will be sent when reports are ready.
