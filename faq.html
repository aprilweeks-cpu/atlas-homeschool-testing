Frequently Asked Questions

ATLAS Homeschool Testing

Registration

Who can participate in ATLAS homeschool testing?

Students in grades 3–10 who are registered by the deadline.

What happens if I miss the registration deadline?

Students who are not registered by the deadline cannot be added to the testing system for the current administration.

Testing

Can my student receive help during testing?

No. Students must complete the assessment independently. Parents may assist with setup only.

What do students need on testing day?

Students need a testing device, internet access, camera, and any allowed materials (scratch paper, calculator).

After Testing

When will scores be available?

Scores are typically available in the ATLAS Family Portal later in the year. Instructions will be sent when reports are ready.
