Before the Session Begins

  • Students should log in at the scheduled time
  • All testing materials (scratch paper, calculator if allowed) should be ready
  • Camera and microphone must be working

During Check-In

  • Students will join a testing session using the provided Test Session ID
  • Students will verify their name on the screen
  • A camera and microphone check will be completed
  • Students must remain present and attentive during this process

During Testing

  • Students must remain on camera for the entire session
  • Microphones must remain on unless instructed otherwise
  • Students must follow all directions from the Test Administrator
  • No outside help is allowed during testing
  • Students may use the chat or raise hand feature if they need assistance
Important:
Any assistance provided during testing may invalidate the student's assessment.

Ending the Test

  • Students should not pause or submit their test unless instructed
  • The Test Administrator will provide directions for ending the session
  • Students must remain in the session until dismissed

Parent Guidance

  • Parents may assist with setup and login only
  • Parents must not help with answering test questions
  • Parents should ensure the testing environment remains quiet

For information about score reports and next steps, please review the After Testing page.
